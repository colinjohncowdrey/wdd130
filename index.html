Colin Cowdrey | WDD 130.

I was born in England and immigrated to Australia with my parents when I was 6 years old. My parents joined the church when I was two years old and we were sealed in the London temple. I graduated from the Elizabeth Boys Technical High School. I served a mission in the New Zealand Christchurch Mission. I am married and have five children, all boys. Each of them have served a mission. After my mission I studied a Bachelor of Arts degree in Accounting. I received by degree and became a Certified Public Accountant. I have worked both Private and Government organisations. I retired in 2024 and am now serving as a full time senior missionary working in the Learning Centre in Lae in Papua New Guinea.

Papua New Guinea

Morobe is a province on the northern cost of Papua New Guinea. The capital of Morobe and largest city is Lae, which is the major sea port for Papua New Guinea. It includes the Huon Peninsula, the Markham River, and delta, and coastal territories along the Huon Gulf.
